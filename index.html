Nike Air Mag
Back To the Future

Nearly fifty years after its conception in 1971, Nikes iconic Swoosh logo remains unchanged, showcasing a prevalent and pioneering symbol for the brand. Perhaps the most sought-after sneaker of all time, the Nike Mag from Back to the Future Part II was released in 2011 via limited eBay auctions to support the Michael J. Fox Foundation. The pair feature a round toe, a flat rubber sole, a signature Nike swoosh, a mid-calf length, a branded ankle strap and light-up detailing.

Nike
Air Yeezy 2 Red October

The Nike Air Yeezy 2 Red October was created in collaboration with musician and fashion designer, Kanye West.The Air Yeezy 2 Red October features a distinctive design that includes a molded upper with a reptilian texture. The midsole is composed of Phylon foam, which is lightweight and flexible, providing cushioning for the foot. The Air-Sole unit is in the heel and is visible through a window in the sole, and provides additional cushioning and support. A strap at the front of the shoe helps to secure the foot in place, while the rubber outsole provides traction on a variety of surfaces.The outsole of the Nike Air Yeezy 2 Red October is made of a durable rubber material with a unique pattern that provides excellent traction on a variety of surfaces. We think that the most notable feature of the shoe is the striking red colorway. The bright red hue is bold and eye-catching, making the sneakers stand out. The shoe was released on February 9th, 2014, at a retail price of $250.
