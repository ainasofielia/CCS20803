Jordan 1 Retro High OG Spider-Man Across the Spider-Verse

RM 1000.00

Product Details

Nike and Jordan Brand are returning back to the Spider-Verse for their second Spider-Man themed Air Jordan 1, with the release of the Air Jordan 1 High OG Spider-Man Across the Spider-Verse.Also known as the Next Chapter, the Jordan 1 High OG Spider-Man Across the Spider-Verse is the next iteration of the original Air Jordan 1 Chicago colorway. The limited edition sneakers are constructed using various leathers, suedes, and other premium materials across the uppers. The patterns used across the sneakers are designed to mimic the Miles Morales/Spider-Man animation style.
