New Balance 9060 Mushroom

RM 700.OO

Product Details

The sneaker features a porous mesh base with several suede overlays stretching from the front to the back. The mesh base takes on dove grey and light pink colors, with the latter hue appearing on the lateral side. Varying shades of mushroom-like tone engage the more extensive overlays along and across the upper, while the N symbol and the accents around the heel portray a simple grey aspect. New Balance offered an off-white/grey rugged sole unit on the underfoot to complete the classic design.
