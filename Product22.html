Adidas Yeezy Foam RNR Stone Salt

RM 550.00

Product Details

The Stone Salt adidas Yeezy Foam RNR showcases a predominantly neutral colorway, with a Stone Salt upper – a blend of gray and beige. The shoe is constructed with a one-piece foam upper, promoting a sock-like fit and lightweight feel. It lacks traditional laces and relies on elastic bands for a secure fit. Carefully positioned vents of different sizes enhance breathability and simultaneously enhance the eye-catching visual appeal of the slip-on design. On the underside, the outsole boasts a wavy traction pattern, ensuring dependable grip.
